Limited Defence

Master the art of strategic defense with limited resources against endless waves of enemies.

Game Overview

Limited Defence is an engaging tower defense strategy game that challenges your resource management and tactical planning skills. Unlike traditional tower defense games, you'll need to make critical decisions about your defense strategy with limited resources available.

Place your defense towers strategically to protect your base from increasingly difficult waves of enemies. Each level presents unique challenges and terrain layouts that require adaptive strategies.

Game Features

  • Multiple tower types with unique abilities and attack patterns
  • Strategic resource management system
  • Progressively challenging levels with diverse enemy types
  • Upgrade system to enhance your defensive capabilities
  • Intuitive controls optimized for both desktop and mobile play

How to Play

  1. Start each level with a limited budget to place defense towers
  2. Analyze the enemy path and determine strategic positions for your towers
  3. Different towers have different costs, attack ranges, and damage output
  4. Earn resources by defeating enemies to build more towers or upgrade existing ones
  5. Plan ahead for later waves which will feature stronger and faster enemies
  6. Prevent enemies from reaching your base to avoid losing lives

Tips and Strategies

  • Focus on placing towers at chokepoints to maximize their effectiveness
  • Diversify your defense with different tower types to counter various enemy abilities
  • Prioritize upgrading existing towers in key positions before building new ones
  • Don't spend all your resources at once - save some for unexpected challenges
  • Pay attention to the preview of upcoming enemy waves to prepare adequately

Technical Requirements

Limited Defence is optimized to run smoothly across different devices:

  • Works on desktop browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Mobile-friendly with touch controls for iOS and Android devices
  • No download required - play instantly in your browser
  • Stable internet connection recommended for uninterrupted gameplay
  • Supports both landscape and portrait orientations on mobile
